What is GapBridge

As qualified and experienced teachers, we've seen a decline in the performance of pupils, during the last two decades. This can be attributed largely to gaps in their foundational knowledge and understanding of subjects such as Mathematics and English.

These gaps are aggrevated as pupils progress from year to year, since very little effective remedial work can be done. Consequently the enjoyment of mastering a subject is lacking among many pupils.

We identify the areas of weakness and we use a literacy and numeracy programme to rectify the problems.

Who are we

GapBridge is presently based in Pretoria.

Included in our field of experience is primary, secondary and tertiary level education, as well as involvement in homeschooling in a personal and professional capacity. We also served as principals for 15 years, collectively.

Our field of experience included 6 years of teacher training in Africa and India.

We've been operational since 2014, with a focus on students struggling academically.

Services we offer

For pricing and fees, please contact us.

Individual assessments
Pupils are assessed in three major areas of language i.e. Spelling, Reading and Comprehension, to determine their level of competencies English.

In Mathematics up to 22 different basic concepts are assessed to reveal specific weaknesses which need correction.

These assessments are aimed at pupils up to age 15 so that the necessary adjustments can be facilitated before they enter the senior phase at school.
Individual extra classes
Based on the outcome of the individual assessments a unique programme is generated to fill the revealed gaps. This programme is then implemented by means of extra classes where each pupil receives individual attention.
Group assessments
At the request of a school, all the pupils in a grade can be assessed. Individual recommendations are then reported to the staff.
School programme implementations
The full programme is electronically available for schools. Selected teachers implement the programme extramurally for the identified pupils. After six months a follow-up assessment is done by us to ascertain the progress of the pupils on the programme.
