What is GapBridge

As qualified and experienced teachers, we've seen a decline in the performance of pupils, during the last two decades. This can be attributed largely to gaps in their foundational knowledge and understanding of subjects such as Mathematics and English.

These gaps are aggrevated as pupils progress from year to year, since very little effective remedial work can be done. Consequently the enjoyment of mastering a subject is lacking among many pupils.

We identify the areas of weakness and we use a literacy and numeracy programme to rectify the problems.

Who are we

GapBridge is presently based in Pretoria.

Included in our field of experience is primary, secondary and tertiary level education, as well as involvement in homeschooling in a personal and professional capacity. We also served as principals for 15 years, collectively.

Our field of experience included 6 years of teacher training in Africa and India.

We've been operational since 2014, with a focus on students struggling academically.
